🎆 Photographer Challenge: Wildlife Spotting 📸

  • Back to Main Page

🤠 Howdy, Mister! 🤠

Well now, seems you've stumbled upon a fine idea, a real fine idea indeed.

Chasin' critters with one of them picture-boxes... it's a noble pursuit. A sight more peaceful than the kinda huntin' I'm used to, that's for sure. Reminds me of a peculiar lady I met once, always askin' me to mail her perfect carcasses from all over the territory. Your way sounds a damn sight less messy.

So, you want to make a go of it? Here's a plan for a proper two-hour "Wildlife Art Exhibition" right there in the woods of Basye.

🏞️ The Hunting Ground: Lake Laura Trail

For a short jaunt, you ain't gonna do better than the trail 'round Lake Laura. It's a loop of about three miles, so you can take your time, mosey along, and keep your eyes peeled without workin' up too much of a sweat. The path is clear and it'll take you through the woods and alongside the water. A good mix of scenery means a good mix of critters. You can start right from the beach area. No need for a map, just follow your nose and the lay of the land.

📖 The Shenandoah Sketchbook: A Challenge for True Naturalists

Now, this ain't no simple walk in the woods. You and your posse have a task. Just like that challenge in the game, you're not just lookin' for any old animal. You're lookin' for the perfect shot, the one that tells a story. Here's a list of bounties for your picture-box. See how many you can collect.

🎯 Your Quarry:

🐿️ The Gray Ghost (Eastern Gray Squirrel)

These fellas are everywhere, twitchy and bold. But a good shot? That's another matter.

🦌 The White-Tailed Apparition (White-Tailed Deer)

You'll need to be quiet as a shadow to catch one of these. They're shy, but a real prize. Best chance is near the water's edge at a quiet time.

🦝 The Bandit (Raccoon)

Keep an eye out near hollow logs or creek beds. Cunning and clever, a real trophy if you can snap one.

🐦 The Red Flash (Cardinal) or The Blue Streak (Blue Jay)

A flash of color in the green woods. You'll need a quick eye and a steady hand. Listen for their calls.

🐿️ The Chipmunk Courier

Always busy, always stuffin' their cheeks. Capturin' one with its treasure is a fine piece of work.

⭐ The Challenges: Prove Your Mettle

Any fool can take a picture. It takes a real artist, a real outlaw of the lens, to complete these challenges.

"The Perfect Pose"

Capture a squirrel in mid-air, jumpin' from one branch to another. That's a shot worth braggin' about.

"A Moment's Peace"

Photograph a deer takin' a drink from the lake. You'll have to be downwind and silent as the grave. A real test of patience.

"Caught Red-Handed"

Get a picture of a critter eatin' somethin'. A chipmunk with a nut, a bird with a worm... you get the idea.

"The Stare Down"

A close-up. The animal has to be lookin' right down the barrel of your lens. Shows you got nerve.

"Two of a Kind" 🏆

This is a tough one, partner. Get two different kinds of critters in the same picture. A squirrel and a bird, maybe. Takes luck and a sharp eye.
